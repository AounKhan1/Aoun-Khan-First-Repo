Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, marinated chicken, and basmati rice. This dish is a popular choice in South Asian cuisine and is often served with raita or salad.

The combination of tender chicken, saffron-infused rice, and rich spices makes it a favorite for special occasions and family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 cardamoms
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
  • Saffron-infused milk (optional, for color and aroma)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (for cooking rice)

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are slightly browned.
  6. Add yogurt and garam masala, then cook for 10 minutes until the chicken is well-coated.
  7. In a separate pot, boil water with bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamom. Add rice and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  8. In a large pot, layer half of the rice, then add the cooked chicken mixture. Add the remaining rice on top.
  9. Sprinkle fried onions, coriander, mint leaves, and saffron-infused milk over the top.
  10. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes (Dum cooking method).
  11. Fluff the biryani gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
